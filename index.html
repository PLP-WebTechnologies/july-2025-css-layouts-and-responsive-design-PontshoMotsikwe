About Ballroom Dancing

Ballroom dancing is a graceful and elegant form of dance that combines rhythm, movement, and connection between partners. It is enjoyed socially and competitively around the world.

Popular Ballroom Dances

  • Waltz
  • Tango
  • Foxtrot
  • Quickstep
  • Viennese Waltz

Steps to Start Ballroom Dancing

  1. Find a local dance studio or class
  2. Choose a style you would like to learn
  3. Practice basic steps with a partner
  4. Listen to the music and follow the rhythm
  5. Keep practicing and have fun!
